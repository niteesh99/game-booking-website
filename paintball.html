Paintball


"Time to get a shot!"

Paintball is an extreme adventure sports where players use guns loaded with harmless paint capsules to shoot at each other. These paint capsules are small balls filled with non-toxic, biodegradable, water soluble mineral-oil, which can be washed after words. These paintball guns are attached with the CO2 gas filled small cylinder for creating pressure, which throws the balls at a very high speed of about 250-300 feet per second. Once the player gets hit by a paintball he/she is out of the particular round.

Paintball game is the thrill of the hunt, it's the fast paced action, blood-pumping, heart stopping game but 100 percent safe. Come as a group to experience the real thrill of paintball.



Pricing and conditions

Each person 250/- for 1 hr and 60 capsules.
Rate increases with number of capsules and time.

Age restriction:

Above 10yrs.

Time:

4 hours or even less.

Come as a group of 10 to have 5 in each team. If you couldn't make upto 10, try bringing atleast 6 people and the teams will be formed by the guides.
