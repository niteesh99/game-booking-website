Rafting


"Rollin' on the river!"

From a quiet drift through forest wilderness to a white-knuckled, wide-eyed journey down turbulent rapids, rafting covers the full adventure spectrum. We have experienced guides to take you safely through the stunning underworld of the Cave. This rafting adventure is all about having thrills and fun while travelling through the beautiful reaches of the River.

Raft the River with our world-class guides, snake through bush clad canyons and plunge down the 7m Falls into the white knuckle foam below, all in one morning of adrenalin-packed fun. Or, try your hand at sledging - we challenge you to get up personal to our the river at eye level.

Propelled by flippers and a floating board, this is a unique way to get your heart rate up! You also get to hear the myths and legends associated with the area and the river. Dare to visit and soak in the thermal waters of the stream
