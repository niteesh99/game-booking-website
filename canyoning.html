Canyoning


"Chase the falls!"

Canyoning is the ultimate adrenalin adventure. Leaping off waterfalls, sliding down rocks and experiencing forest and river areas that few ever get to see.

It requires all kinds of skills - scrambling, climbing, jumping, sliding, abseiling, swimming and shouting "yeehaa" as often as you dare. It’s a wet business, but you’re equipped with a tough wetsuit that provides warmth.

We now also offer night canyoning. You and your guide will then explore your way through the narrow canyon illuminated only by your headlamp and the glow worms on the walls.



Pricing and conditions

Adult 700/-

Age restriction:

Above 10yrs and 25kg required.

Travel time:

4 hours round or even less.
