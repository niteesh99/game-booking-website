Zip-lining


"Step off the edge!"

The growing trend of zip lining involves an exhilarating mix of adrenaline, speed and nature. This 3-hour wilderness adventure mixes fun and delight together to create unforgettable memories. Glide through the treetops on a series of ziplines with spectacular views.

It takes you down a steep incline at speeds of up to 70km per hour + Drop (1 hour) which is a whole new way to experience the heightened thrill level and includes a swooping 21m drop! It is a guided adventure (2 tour guides) and runs all year round, whatever the weather. Everything is provided to keep you safe and comfortable including harness; safety tethers; overalls; helmets; lights and boots.

The perfect combination of fun and excitement amidst a spectacular forest environment - unmissable. Tree hugger or dare devil, come along with your friends or family and share a great adventure.



Pricing and conditions

Adult 1000/-

Age restriction:

Above 7yrs and 25kg required.

Travel time:

3 hours or even less.
