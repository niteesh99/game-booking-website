Rappelling


"Chase the cliffs!"

Abseiling, or rappelling, is an adventure activity suitable for all skill levels. Get a thrill rappelling down a rock face, waterfall or even into a cave!The sheer thrill of a controlled descent down a rope from a cliff will transform your sense of gravity. You will have the chance to rappel the vertical parts of the mountain with natural as well as artificial grips for newbies.

Controlled descent is an enthralling way to interact with the landscape. And the great thing about abseiling, is that it’s quite easy to learn. After a few lessons, you’ll be slipping down the rope like a master.

The skilled guides and instructors who operate abseiling experiences know all about easing people out of their comfort zone, so that you get an amazing sense of accomplishment.



Pricing and conditions

Adult 500/-

Age restriction:

Above 7yrs and 25kg required.

Travel time:

3 hours or even less.
