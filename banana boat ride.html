Banana Boat Ride


"Resist more!"

Banana boat is an inflatable boat that is shaped just like a banana and connected to a speedboat that pulls it along the water at high speeds. Everyone riding the banana boat should hold on as tight as possible as the speedboat attempts to flip the inflatable boat,dropping its passengers into water.

It is fun and super exciting for all age, whether it is little children or elderly people. Life jackets are given for safety. Banana ride is a recreational activity that relaxes your mind and body completely.



Pricing and conditions

Adult 1500/- per hour.

Age restriction:

Above 10yrs and 25kg required.

Travel time:

5 hours.

Come as a group of 4 to 6.


"Clutch on tight to return in triumph!!"
