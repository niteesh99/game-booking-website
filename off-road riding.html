Off-road riding


"Challenge the terrain!"

Hurtling down dirt roads on a mountain bike and getting a big thrill out of it is for the hardy and the fittest adventure seekers. Some off-road riding tracks are gravel, some are dirt and others are not much more than a sheep trail up the hillside. Off-roading can be a self-drive scoot around the forest on a quad bike.

Ride through lush green rice fields, delightful little hamlets, up and down the hills, along river banks, around temples, past the stray cattle, over suspension bridges and along the highway. So buckle up, start your engine and hold on tight, the scenic wilderness is waiting.



Pricing and conditions

Adult 500/-

Age restriction:

Above 13yrs.

Travel time:

2 hours round or even less.
