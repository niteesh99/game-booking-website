Bungee Jumping


"3, 2, 1 Bungy!"

Take the ultimate plunge with our Bungee from the 120m high platform. Free fall for 8.5 seconds in a blitz of pure fear and adrenaline. With heaps of different special jump styles that might pop out every now and then, there’s a fun or scary style for you. Which ever way you jump, launch or get released, there’s a fun or scary style for you.

The Swoop consists of specially designed hang gliding harnesses where you are cocooned inside. It can take one, two or three people at any one time, the more people the better the ride. One lucky person will be nominated to pull the ripcord on command and you are winched 40 meters sky high above the park and surrounding adventure rides. Your jumpmaster will call ‘ready on the ripcord’ and as you release yourself, you plunge towards the earth at 130kmph. This is definitely a ride that will get your heart pumping!

This is one of the best valued bungee jumpings in India. Laughter and screaming is guaranteed on this adrenaline experience. Your experience will be ultimately captured, so you can relive the moment over and over again.
